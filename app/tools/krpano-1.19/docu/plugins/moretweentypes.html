MoreTweenTypes Plugin moretweentypes.swf / moretweentypes.js

The moretweentypes plugin extends krpano with the following tween types:

Ease Out and In:
  • easeOutInQuart
  • easeOutInQuint
  • easeOutInSine
  • easeOutInBounce
  • easeOutInCirc
  • easeOutInCubic
  • easeOutInExpo
Ease In and Out:
  • easeInOutQuad
  • easeInOutQuart
  • easeInOutQuint
  • easeInOutSine
  • easeInOutBounce
  • easeInOutCirc
  • easeInOutCubic
  • easeInOutExpo

Syntax

Include the plugin once by: 
<plugin url.html5="moretweentypes.js"
        url.flash="moretweentypes.swf"
    	preload="true"
        />
